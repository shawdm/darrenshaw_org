Lachlan Bailey had a previous shoot with Margot Robbie that I loved. I mainly remember it for its dreamy Summer colours
and Margot having a full-fat can of Coke.
This [Vogue Australia shoot](https://www.vogue.com.au/culture/features/margot-robbie-jacob-elordi-vogue-cover/image-gallery/5ad9d7f5993076606fd750152a3678fc)
is in a very similar style. In theory it's of both Margot Robbie and Jacob Elordi but really there is only Margot in it.
