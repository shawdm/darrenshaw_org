My favourite photographer is [Peter Lindbergh](https://peterlindbergh.obys.agency). Among the many things I love about
his pictures is that he didn't try to make his subjects look younger than they were. He just photographed people in the
most natural and beautiful way (see his
[2017 Pirelli Calendar](https://www.vogue.co.uk/article/pirelli-calendar-2017-stars) for example). Greg Williams'
pictures of Gillian Anderson are different, but they still remind me of Lindbergh.
